# API Specifications

This page contains the API specifications for an array of the **V1.2 Open Finance capabilities**. It provides detailed descriptions of the APIs that enable secure, consent-driven access to banking data, payments, and product information.

## Bank Data Sharing APIs (v1.2)

The **Bank Data Sharing APIs (v1.2)** enable secure access to customer banking data with consent. They allow retrieval of:

- Account details  
- Balances  
- Transactions  
- Beneficiaries  
- Direct debits  
- Standing orders  
- Scheduled payments  
- Product information  

Additionally, they provide information about account holders and related parties.  



## Payment Initiation APIs (v1.2)

The **Payment Initiation APIs (v1.2)** enable secure initiation and management of customer payments with consent. They support:

- Creating and managing payment consents  
- Initiating single payments  
- Retrieving payment status  
- Handling refunds  
- Bulk or file-based payments and reporting  

These APIs form the foundation of open finance payment services, enabling trusted third parties to initiate and track payments securely on behalf of customers.  



## Products and Leads APIs

The **Products and Leads APIs** enable secure and unified access to available product data. They provide:

- Structured, up-to-date information on features, rates, eligibility, and terms  
- Functionality to create and post leads, allowing seamless product applications  

These APIs form the foundation of open finance product journeys, enabling trusted third parties to present product options and manage lead submissions securely on behalf of customers.



## Confirmation of Payee API (v1.2)

The **Confirmation of Payee API (v1.2)** enables secure verification of payee account details within the UAE Open Finance framework. It supports:

- Confirmation of account holder names against provided identifiers (e.g., IBANs)  
- Discovery operations to identify the appropriate Licensed Financial Institution (LFI) for verification  
- Confirmation operations to validate payee details  


This ensures accurate and secure payment processing.
